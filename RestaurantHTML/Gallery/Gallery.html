The Midnight Caviar Affair

A once-a-month, invitation-only event where guests indulge in the world’s rarest caviars, paired with vintage champagnes and handcrafted ice sculptures that serve the dishes. The event begins precisely at midnight, with a blind tasting led by a Michelin-starred chef.

Dress Code-

Black-tie with a hint of avant-garde. Think Tom Ford velvet jackets, sequined gowns, and diamond cufflinks.

Notable Guests-

Wall Street moguls, European royalty, and A-list celebrities

The Baccarat Dinner

A lavish dinner where every dish is served in Baccarat crystalware, and the wine is poured from custom-cut decanters worth more than most penthouses. The highlight of the evening is the Dorsia Signature Wagyu Tartare, prepared tableside with gold leaf and Beluga caviar.

Dress Code-

Opulent formalwear—bespoke tuxedos, haute couture gowns, and vintage watches worth six figures.

Notable Guests-

CEOs, Hollywood producers, and the occasional reclusive billionaire.

The Obsidian Soirée

A mysterious black-themed gala where all guests must wear noir-inspired attire. The venue is bathed in candlelight, and the menu features dark truffle soufflés, squid ink risotto, and blackened Chilean sea bass. Classical musicians play haunting compositions as masked waiters serve rare cocktails infused with black garlic and saffron.

Dress Code-

Monochrome black only, preferably custom designer pieces.

Notable Guests-

Hedge fund elites, luxury fashion designers, and infamous art collectors.

The Vanishing Vintage Wine Tasting

A highly exclusive event featuring wines so rare that only a single bottle remains in existence. The highlight is the legendary Château Lafite 1787, once rumored to be owned by Thomas Jefferson. Guests bid on sips, and the highest bidder earns the privilege of tasting history.

Dress Code-

White dinner jackets for men, deep jewel-toned evening gowns for women.

Notable Guests-

Secretive wine collectors, legacy families, and diplomats.

The Seven Sins Banquet

A seven-course meal where each dish is themed after one of the seven deadly sins. Highlights include Gluttony (a foie gras-stuffed pheasant with a saffron-infused reduction) and Lust (a dark chocolate and chili soufflé with gold-dusted raspberries). Each course is paired with an equally extravagant cocktail.

Dress Code-

Extravagant decadence—anything that screams wealth and indulgence.

Notable Guests-

Wall Street elites, film stars, and anyone on the Forbes 400 list.

The Haute Secret Society Dinner

An invite-only, anonymous event where guests arrive in Venetian masks, and their names are never revealed. The entire menu is composed of undisclosed, experimental dishes crafted by the world's top chefs. Guests must rely on their senses, as no descriptions are provided.

Dress Code-

Full black tie, with masks that must be uniquely designed for the occasion.

Notable Guests-

Thinkers, inventors, and the world’s most powerful unknown figures.
